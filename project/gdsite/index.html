GD MFA Exhibition

The website is for an exhibition of graduating Graphic Design MFA students. Each person designed a 12 foot long panel to be displayed in the gallery and the website takes those panels and makes one long scrolling website out of them. The experience when viewing them online, especially on wide monitors, is completely different than the experience of viewing them in person. The site creates a new interaction with the same material by shifting the scale.
